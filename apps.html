Android Apps

Android Apps that help you protect your online privacy on your smartphone.

Jumbo

The Jumbo app automatically deletes search histories, searches for email address leaks and protects you from having your privacy breached through your phone.

Exodus Privacy

Exodus privacy scans apps on your phone for trackers and apps that are sending data to third parties and allows you to easily see apps that are breaching your privacy through your phone.
