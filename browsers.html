Browsers

To secure your data privacy while browsing the internet.

Firefox

Probably the most well known privacy browser, Firefox effectively protects your privacy by blocking trackers and spyware. It is known for it being an open-source browser, allowing anyone to see how it works.

Brave

The Brave privacy browser has many features for protecting your privacy while browsing the internet, along with all of it's privacy features it also encourages viewing ads that respect your privacy and are not targeted. It rewards you credits for viewing privacy-respecting ads that you can save up and donate directly to charities or exchange them for real money.

Epic

The Epic privacy browser has the most privacy protecting features with built-in adblockers, antivirus, VPNs, antitrackers and much, much more. The downside is that it forfeits a lot of user-friendliness. It is a bit more inconvinient to use than other browsers and it does not have save any browsing history, even locally.

Avast Secure

Avast Secure privacy browser is fantastic for the average Chrome user that wants more privacy online. It comes with the run-of-the-mill privacy features like antiviruses and antitrackers, and it looks almost identical to Chrome all while being very user-friendly and easy to use.
