Organizations

A great way to advocate for the open internet and online privacy is by supporting organizations and charities to take action.
Below are three organizations dedicated to fighting for internet freedom and online privacy rights.

  • Electronic Frontier Foundation
  • Privacy International
  • International Association of Privacy Professionals

Electronic Frontier Foundation

The Electronic Frontier Foundation is a veteran organization in the community of internet freedom and data privacy protection. The organization serves many purposes from legal battles for internet freedom and spreading the word about data privacy, to organizing events and activities advocating for the subject.

Privacy International

Privacy International is an organization that participates in legal cases to help protect online privacy against governments or companies. They also have a fantastic Youtube channel with great informational videos explaining big data, security and a wide range of other topics.

International Association of Privacy Professionals

The International Association of Privacy Proffesionals primarily focused on training and education people to learn how to implement cybersecurity to protect data privacy to prevent security breaches and identity theft.
