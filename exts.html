Extensions

Chrome extensions made to minimize tracking of your data online.

Privacy Badger

Privacy Badger is an extension made by the Electronic Frontier Foundation that will automatically block cookies on web pages that try to track your data. It conviniently tells you what cookies and how many cookies it blocked for you.

HTTPS Everywhere

HTTPS Everywhere is another extension made by the Electronic Frontier Foundation that forces a safe HTTPS connection on every website, reducing the risks of unsafe websites that try to track you.

Ghostery

Ghostery blocks dangerous cookies and trackers, and it comes with ad blocking and it includes a very detailed report of what it blocks and how dangerous the site you are on may be.

Netcraft

The Netcraft extension gives you a risk evaluation for websites you visit as well as a rating that shows in a very simple and convinient way how dangerous a site may be. It is made to help prevent phishing attacks from untrustworthy sites.
